Thank you for participating in this experiment

The experiment: In this experiment, you will be given several questions in which each question contains a picture of many Youtube thumbnails videos. You will be asked to select one of the thumbnail videos that you think you will likely click. The setup of the picture tries to replicate the Youtube recommendation system with some simplification.

Answering questions: You can select the thumbnail video by selecting the order number of the thumbnail. In selecting the best thumbnail, you should consider the title and the image of each video thumbnail. An answer is always required and only one thumbnail can be chosen. There are no right or wrong answers here beyond your intuition. You should answer each question as quickly as you can but without rushing. The survey would take around 5 minutes.

Example questions: The experiment will be started by 3 example questions to help you get familiar with the setup.

Purpose of the research: This experiment is conducted under the course ‘Experimentation in Psychology and Linguistics 2020’ at Utrecht University, The Netherlands. This experiment aims to collect data and analyze human behavior in choosing a Youtube video recommendation. The exact behavior being tested cannot be shared with the participant to avoid any influence on the experiment.

Consent in participation: By completing this survey, you are giving informed consent to provide data for research and education purposes, as specified in the information sheet below. You are free to withdraw from the experiment. The experimenters will pre-process the data anonymously and confidentially. The data we collect, in an anonymous and aggregated format, may be made available for research and teaching purposes. Research results may be published in journal articles, conference presentations and via any other mode of scientific exchange and dissemination that will be seen as appropriate by the researchers while protecting the participants’ anonymity. Voluntary participation and freedom to withdraw from the research your choice to take part in the research is entirely voluntary. You are completely free to choose not to participate, or to withdraw from the study at any moment without any consequence. For any further doubt or clarification request, do not hesitate to ask!
